Logo Highlights

FC Chitwan

A regular in Nepal Super League (NSL), FC Chitwan brings the enthusiasm from Chitwan to the playground. With the mascot of panther instead of the traditional rhino, FC Chitwan has the hunger to win it all!

Vairav

“Gurkhas of the cyber security world”, Vairav works in the field of cyber security offering various services to its client. Vairav name is derived from the ancient deity who is a powerful manifestation of Lord Shiva according to the Hindu mythology and the logo depicts the head of the deity.

Buneko

Buneko (nepali for woven) is a brand in Nepal that promotes sustainable fashion contrary to the world of fast fashion. Buneko mainly deals with surplus clothes which allows them to provide its customer with high end clothes in a reasonable price.
